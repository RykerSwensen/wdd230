Balance

FlyHyer

www.flyhyer.com

FlyHyer is a great example of using balance in their website. They utiliza a fun visual of an aircraft in the air, with subtle clouds that faid into the white background of the site. An aircraft is a very unique and bold piece of machinery, it is not easy to balance your elements of a website with the image of an aircraft. However, with the sizing and placement of the aircraft, along with the complimentary sizement and arrangement of the websites elements, you are left with a beautifully balanced website. I learned about balance here: www.youtube.com

Color

FPP

fpp.net

FPP uses a complimentary color pallet throughout their site. The nuetral samon pink background highlights the white shelves and yellow products on display. With all these bright colors, the site is still not too bright or too colorful. All of the colors compliment while not taking away from eachother. I learned about color here: www.youtube.com

Emphasis and Contrast

Superlist

www.superlist.com

Superlist does a great job using emphasis and contrast. As soon as you open the site, it grabs your attention by dividing the dark navy background with a hot red as you navigate the site. These two colors contrast in a way to grab your attention. Navy blue and hot red are not necissarily complimentary, nor are they abrasive and hard to look at. It is contrast designed to be fun and pull your attention. I learned about contrast and emphasis here: www.youtube.com
